A simple tool to help your organization stay safer online

Answer honest questions about your current practices - no judgment, just helpful guidance!

About This Assessment

This tool evaluates your organization's security awareness maturity across eight critical domains. Based on research with small businesses and nonprofits, it provides practical, actionable recommendations tailored to resource-constrained environments.

Who should take this: Anyone familiar with how your organization operates—owner, manager, IT person, or long-time employee.

How it works: The assessment takes approximately 10-15 minutes to complete. Answer honestly based on your organization's current practices—not what you aspire to do. Your responses will generate a customized security improvement roadmap with simple, practical steps explained in plain English.

Assessment Domains: Formal Training, Phishing Awareness, Policies & Procedures, Incident Response, Multi-Factor Authentication & Passwords, BYOD Practices, Leadership Engagement, and Security Communications.
